  • Substantive Editing

    • $40 - $75/page

      Also referred to as developmental editing, substantive editing involves taking a step back from a manuscript to ask several important questions:

      How does the novel flow from one section to the next?

      Are the themes consistent?

      Is there unnecessary exposition that should be omitted?

      Which chapters are weakest and could benefit from rewriting? ​

      These and more. Everything which could affect the quality of a novel on the macro level will be explored. This type of editing is extensive, and can occasionally lead to rewriting entire sections of a manuscript. The author will be intimately involved with every decision made during this process.
